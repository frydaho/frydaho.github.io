In classic middle-aged-coder style, I find myself drawn to honoring the "Hello World!" in implementing this site. I can't speak for other devs, but for many years as a younger coder I rejected the idea of publishing anything with the words "Hello World" in it.

What's different today is that I'm at a point where I truly *grok* the code I write; I look back and see that it all started with being willing to embrace the "hello world" of the language. I honor that I could have never gotten to **this** point without beginning at **that** point. I honor the "hello world" moments in my life every day because they are the *commencement* of my future greatness.

So, in that vein, "Hello World!"
